About Me

Hello, and welcome to my website! I decided I would give making one a try over some long weekends. I've always been fond of personal blogs, so I wanted to create something similar for the 'portfolio' section of this site. This will hopefully create a more familar feeling for you, the viewer, when browsing though my project archives.

If you're here to learn the basics about me, I'm a 3A Systems Design Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. My main interests include product-based research and design, which tie in nicely with my studies. What I enjoy most are collaborations. I also really love learning about what others are interested in. Feel free to shoot me an e-mail!
